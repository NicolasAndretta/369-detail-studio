# 369 Detail — Business Rules

## Business Philosophy

369 Detail prioritizes:

- quality over quantity
- premium perception
- customer trust
- visual transformation
- attention to detail

The business must never feel:

- cheap
- rushed
- generic
- mass-production oriented

The customer experience should feel personalized and premium.

---

# Customer Experience Standards

## Core Principles

Every client interaction should communicate:

- professionalism
- trust
- transparency
- confidence
- premium care

The client must feel:

- their vehicle matters
- the work is specialized
- the result is worth paying for

---

# Appointment Logic

## Booking System

The business currently operates primarily through:

- WhatsApp inquiries
- manual scheduling
- personalized attention

The website should NOT behave like an automated booking platform.

The experience should feel human and direct.

---

## Turn Management

Turn scheduling should support:

- limited simultaneous vehicles
- service availability control
- personalized communication

The business prioritizes service quality over excessive booking volume.

---

# Service Philosophy

## Positioning

369 Detail is not a standard car wash.

Services should be communicated as:

- premium automotive care
- vehicle transformation
- paint enhancement
- protection and restoration
- aesthetic preservation

The language should elevate perceived value.

---

# Pricing Philosophy

## Premium Positioning

Pricing communication should emphasize:

- quality
- precision
- professionalism
- transformation value

Avoid:

- discount-focused messaging
- cheap positioning
- aggressive price competition

The brand should attract quality-focused customers.

---

# WhatsApp Conversion Rules

## Primary Conversion Goal

The main CTA across the website is WhatsApp contact.

Important sections should naturally direct users toward:

- requesting information
- asking for availability
- starting a conversation
- scheduling a service

---

## CTA Style

CTA language should feel:

- direct
- premium
- concise
- action-oriented

Examples:

- Transformá tu vehículo
- Solicitar turno
- Hablar por WhatsApp
- Ver resultados

Avoid:

- spammy marketing language
- excessive urgency
- exaggerated promises
